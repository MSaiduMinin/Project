EMPOWERING DREAMS

Green Farm Limited is an agritech company working with farmers to unlock agribusiness opportunities in Africa. With offices in Nigeria and Kenya, we provide end to end innovative market edge agribusiness solutions to farmers throughout the continent. Our philosophy is anchored on creating business opportunities for farmers through developing quality products and services as well as new markets. Our story is imprinted on excellence and quality that ushers a new world of wealth creation through agribusiness while leveraging the power of agricultural technology.

WINNING TOGETHER

As a company, our mission goes beyond achieving the financial bottom-line. In this respect, our values are anchored on being experts in our field, building viable partnerships for agricultural transformation, sharing knowledge with others, and reaching more farmers than we can alone, ensuring effective technologies are widely accessed. We believe in ecological stewardship, operational excellence, a partnership approach, and technical know-how to drive prosperity among farmers in Africa while profitably sustaining our business.

REASONS TO CHOOSE US

To guarantee and inspire the confidence of all our clients and partners on the reliability of our solutions, proprietary innovations by Green Farm Limited have been thoroughly researched, tried and tested before roll out. Our promise is the commitment to providing innovative market edge agribusiness solutions to farmers throughout Sub-Saharan Africa. Supported by our team of seasoned professionals, we aim to maximize improvements in society as well as environmental well-being among farmers through commercial business operations. Our consultancy rates are very competitive and you will assuredly prosper with us.
